## Home of the Rustacean Station podcast

Rustacean Station is a community project for creating podcast content
for the Rust programming language. Come journey with us into the weird,
wonderful, and wily world of Rust.

If you would like to offer Rust-related podcast content for us to host,
or would like advice and resources on making your own Rust podcast, get
in touch with us via the venues below!

 - Twitter: [@rustaceanfm](https://twitter.com/rustaceanfm)
 - Discord: [Rustacean Station](https://discord.gg/cHc3Gyc)
 - Email: [hello@rustacean-station.org](mailto:hello@rustacean-station.org)

## Instructions

The intro jingle: https://audio.rustacean-station.org/file/rustacean-station/intro.mp3

Using audacity:

* Import the intro at the start.
* Import your the recording.
* Fade it the intro when you start talking. Leave roughly 10 sec intro. Select the fadin area. Click on Effect/fading/Fade Out.
* Click on the intro at the timestamp where it faded out. Right-click. Split Clip. Right click. Cut.
* Volume normalization (often just an option in the export step) set "-16 LUFS". That just ensures that your audio file is the same volume as our other episodes. Ctrl-A/Effect/Volume and Compression/  Normalize preceived loudness to -16.0 LUFS / Apply
* Export as mono mp3 with a bitrate of 96kbps. File/Export Audio/(Channels: Mono | Quality 96 kbps)/Export
